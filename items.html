Our Story

It would be safe to say we, here at Betty's Boutique, are obsessed with the Roaring 20's.

We would spend hours scouring Ebay, Craigslist, and Etsy

We thought that it would be amazing to have one place to go to find real 20's items.

Then we had the idea of doing the work so you wouldn't have to. Let us search for you. We're doing it anyway.
